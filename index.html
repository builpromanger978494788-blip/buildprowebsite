Transparent Workflow

Your Journey with Us

Building a home can be stressful. Our clear, 4-step process ensures you are always informed and in control.

1

Initial Consultation & Site Visit

We meet to understand your vision, budget, and requirements. Our experts visit your plot to analyze soil, dimensions, and Vastu directions.

2

Design & Approval

Our architects create customized Vastu-compliant floor plans and 3D elevations. We revise them until you are 100% satisfied, then get necessary municipal approvals.

3

Construction & Updates

Construction begins with top-grade materials. You receive regular progress reports, photos, and site visit invitations to track the development.

4

Quality Check & Handover

After a rigorous multi-point quality inspection of plumbing, electricals, and finishes, we hand over the keys to your dream home.
